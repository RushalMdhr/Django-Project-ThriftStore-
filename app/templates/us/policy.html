Return & Exchange Policy - Thrift Store

We understand that sometimes things don't work out. Please read our return & exchange policy below for more details on how we can help.

Eligibility for Return

Items can be returned within 14 days of purchase. The items must be in the same condition as when they were purchased, with all original tags and packaging intact. If the item is faulty or damaged, we will accept a return or exchange.

How to Return

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service at support@thriftstore.com with your order details. We will provide you with a return label and instructions for returning your item.

Refunds

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 7-10 business days after the returned item has been received and inspected. Please note that shipping fees are non-refundable.

Exchanges

If you wish to exchange an item, we will process the exchange as soon as we receive the original item back in the return condition. If the item is out of stock, we will offer a store credit or a full refund.

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene reasons, items such as underwear, swimwear, and certain accessories cannot be returned. Additionally, gift cards, sale items, and items marked as final sale are non-returnable.
