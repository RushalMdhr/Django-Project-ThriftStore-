Frequently Asked Questions

When can I expect my order to arrive?

Each gown is crafted to order. Delivery typically takes 3–8 weeks, including production, handling, and shipping. During high-volume events, there may be delays.

Is there an option for expedited delivery?

Some dresses are available with expedited shipping. Choose the desired shipping option during checkout.

Which delivery service do you use?

We dispatch products globally via DHL Express courier service.

Are import taxes and duties included?

Customs duties depend on your country's regulations. These fees are collected by DHL and are not included in the product price.

How can I contact you about my order?

You can reach us by email at budhathokimalika@gmail.com. We’ll respond as soon as possible.

What is the return policy?

We accept returns for unworn, standard-size items within 7 days of delivery. Customized items are non-refundable. Shipping charges are non-refundable.

Is there a delay for custom size orders?

Custom size and standard gowns take 3–8 weeks for delivery, depending on the shipping option selected at checkout.
